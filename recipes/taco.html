Cuban Taco's

Home

Description

A sweet take on traditional tacos. Although adding cranberry's may sound odd at first, once you try these you will never make normal tacos again.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb-ground beef
  • Hot taco seasoning packet
  • Cranberry sauce
  • Craisins
  • Molasses

Steps

  1. Brown 1 lb of ground beef
  2. Add hot seasoning mix
  3. Stir in cranberry sauce and molasses to taste
  4. Add in craisins
  5. Serve in taco shells and with any toppings you like
