Creamy Tomato Soup

Home

Description

A delicious cream based tomato soup that will have your mouth watering at just the thought of it. One of my favorite dishes my wife has made for me. Pairs great with a nice warm grilled cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 can-crushed tomatoes
    • 1 box of chicken broth
  • 1 tsp- oregano
  • 1 tsp- basil
  • 2 cups- heavy whipping cream
