Creamy Tomato Soup

Home

Description

A delicious cream based tomato soup that you'll want to have day after day. One of my favorite dishes my wife has made for me. Pairs great with a nice warm grilled cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 can-crushed tomatoes
    • 1 box of chicken broth
  • 1 tsp- oregano
  • 1 tsp- basil
  • 2 cups- heavy whipping cream
