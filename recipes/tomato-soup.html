Creamy Tomato Soup

Description

A delicious cream based tomato soup that you'll want to have day after day. One of my favorite dishes my wife has made for me. Pairs great with a nice warm grilled cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 can-crushed tomatoes
    • 1 box of chicken broth
  • 1 tsp- oregano
  • 1 tsp- basil
  • 2 cups- heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Add can of crushed tomatoes and a box of chicken broth to medium sized crock-pot
    2. Measure out a tsp of oregano and a tsp of basil and mix in
  2. Let sit on low for 8 hours
  3. Once 8 hours is up turn heat off
  4. Mix in 2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  5. Serve hot with a delicious grilled cheese sandwhich
